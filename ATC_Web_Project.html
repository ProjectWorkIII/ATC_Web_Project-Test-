Introduction to Australian Training Company

Who Is Australian Training Company?

The Australian Training Company (ATC) is a not-for-profit organization that provides training and employment opportunities for students, apprentices, and trainees. Established in 1994, ATC is a Group Training Organisation (GTO) and a Registered Training Organisation (RTO).

What Does ATC offer?

Flexible study arrangements

ATC offers flexible education programs that can be tailored to meet individual needs.

Learning platforms

ATC provides online and distance learning platforms for study materials and assessments.
